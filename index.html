Hacker News Zero

A minimalist Hacker News app for iOS

A Minimalist App For Hacker News

Tired of the messy clutter of today's news apps? Sick of apps pestering you to mention them on countless amounts of social networks? Do you just want to read the latest tech news? Well then Hacker News Zero is for you!

iPhone and iPad Support

Hacker News Zero has full support for both iPhone and iPad, as well as iOS 6 and iOS 7. So get Hacker News Zero on your iPhone for your on-the-go needs, and Hacker News Zero for the iPad for some couch reading!
